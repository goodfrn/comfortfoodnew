ingredients:
- "2 cups Nutella hazelnut spread"
- "⅓ cup neutral vegetable oil (can swap with light olive oil or melted coconut oil)"
- "½ cup granulated sugar (reduced from ¾ cup for less sweetness)"
- "3 large eggs, room temp"
- "1 cup all-purpose flour"
- "1½ teaspoons baking powder (slightly increased)"
- "pinch of fine sea salt"
instructions:
- "Line a 9x9 inch pan with parchment or foil and spray generously. Avoid skipping this or edges and bottom stubbornly stick."
- "Preheat oven to 345°F (175°C). Slightly lower than standard 350 to prevent over-baking edges. Learn to trust sight and jiggle too."
- "In a large bowl, combine Nutella, oil, sugar, eggs. Whisk thoroughly into a shiny, glossy batter. No dry streaks. Use rubber spatula to scrape sides. Take your time here."
- "Fold in flour, baking powder, salt just until fully integrated. No flour patches but resist urge to keep stirring. Overmix equals tough brownies."
- "Pour batter into pan. Batter thick, spreads slowly. Smooth surface with spatula but keep gentle touch."
- "Warm an extra ¼ cup Nutella in microwave about 15 seconds – watch it closely; it should shine not boil. Drop teaspoons across batter then drag a knife through for swirling patterns. Less swirl mixing = more visual impact and gooey pockets."
- "Bake 32 to 40 minutes. Check around 32. Edges will set firmly and pull slightly away. Center jiggles electrically but is not runny when nudged. Too firm? Dry. Too loose? Underbaked."
- "Cool on rack at least 15 minutes before cutting. These brownies firm better at room temp than fridge; refrigeration tightens texture and enhances Nutella aroma but makes cutting tougher."
- "Tip: Use sharp knife, wipe blade after each cut. If swirls look pale — bake a few more minutes. If edges crumble, reduce oven temp or time next batch."
- "Optional twist: Fold in handful of toasted chopped hazelnuts or swirl in tablespoons of salted caramel for added crunch and flavor depth."
