ingredients:
- "2 large eggs"
- "260 ml (1 cup plus 2 tbsp) milk of choice"
- "240 ml (1 cup) corn flour"
- "1.25 ml (1/5 tsp) fine salt"
- "Softened butter or coconut oil for pan"
instructions:
- "Crack eggs into a medium bowl. Whisk them up. Add milk and salt, whisk again."
- "Add corn flour slowly. Keep whisking. You don't want lumps. Batter should be loose, not watery. Add a splash of milk or a spoon of flour if it's off."
- "Heat a nonstick pan to medium. Wait for it to shimmer. Not smoke."
- "Thin layer of butter or coconut oil. Not too much or the edges get greasy."
- "Pour about half a cup of batter. Swirl fast. You want it thin and even. Watch for the edges to dry and bubbles to form."
- "When the bottom is golden and edges lift easy, flip it. Other side takes 20 seconds. Maybe 25."
- "Stack them on a plate. Cover with foil so they stay soft."
- "Batter gets thick sitting around. Splash of milk fixes that."
- "First crepe is a test. Always. Adjust from there."
- "Serve warm. Fill them with whatever you want."
