ingredients:
- "1 cup unsalted butter softened"
- "¾ cup creamy peanut butter"
- "½ cup packed light brown sugar"
- "⅓ cup granulated sugar plus extra for rolling"
- "1 large egg plus 1 yolk at room temp"
- "2 tbsp whole milk or cream"
- "1 tsp pure vanilla extract"
- "1¾ cups all-purpose flour"
- "½ tsp baking soda"
- "½ tsp kosher salt"
- "½ cup semi-sweet chocolate chips or wafers optional for dipping"
- "Festive sprinkles for garnish optional"
instructions:
- "Preheat oven to 345-350F. Line 2 sheets with parchment or silicone mats."
- "Whisk dry: flour, baking soda, salt evenly. Sets the stage."
- "In stand mixer with paddle, beat butter, brown and granulated sugar, peanut butter on med-high 3-4 min. Look for creamy, light, fluffy mass. Scrape bowl walls often to avoid pockets."
- "Add egg, yolk, milk, vanilla. Mix till just combined. Watch texture; avoid overmix."
- "Reduce speed. Gradually add dry mix. Dough will feel airy, not sticky. If overly loose, 10 min fridge rest hydrates flour for easier scooping."
- "Scoop 2 tbsp dough balls. Roll in granulated sugar coating for subtle crunch and shine."
- "Place spaced on sheets. Should yield ~14-16 cookies."
- "Bake 8-12 min, watch edges for light golden, centers still soft and puffed. Slight underbaking locks chewiness as cookies firm off sheet. Cool 5 min to flatten slightly."
- "Shaping trick: swirl cup over warm cookies to round edges."
- "Set on wire rack to cool completely."
- "Melt chocolate in microwave in 25-30 sec bursts, stirring after each till silky smooth."
- "Dip halves of cooled cookies in chocolate, add sprinkles immediately. Let set to harden before serving."
