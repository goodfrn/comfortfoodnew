- "=== Mix & Taste ==="

- "=== Storage & Use ==="
- "Transfer to airtight container immediately, press down slightly to reduce air pockets. Store somewhere dark, room temp. Keeps its crunch and aroma for up to 11 months if dry. If you detect any mustiness or sogginess, toss and make fresh. Use on everything salty—roasted potatoes, egg salad, even popcorn. Sprinkle on and press in with fingers for best adhesion."
