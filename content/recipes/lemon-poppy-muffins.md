ingredients:
- "2 cups self-rising flour, divided (use 1 7/8 and 1 1/8 cups instead)"
- "3/4 cup sugar"
- "1/2 cup melted coconut oil (sub for vegetable oil)"
- "Zest of 2 lemons"
- "1 cup lemon sparkling water, replaced with sparkling apple cider"
- "2 tablespoons poppy seeds"
instructions:
- "===Preheat and Prep==="
- "Heat oven near 375°F. Line muffin tins with liners. Don’t skimp; liners keep muffins moist and cleanup quick."
- "===Mixing Wet and Dry==="
- "In stand mixer or big bowl, toss sugar, melted coconut oil, lemon zest. Mix till grainy sugar dissolves a bit in oil. Feels grainy but fragrant."
- "===Alternate Flour and Liquid==="
- "Add flour and sparkling cider in parts, starting with about 1 7/8 cups flour, then half cider amount. Mix gently, mix just until batters barely combine. Batter should look streaky still — that’s good."
- "===Fold Seeds & Avoid Toughness==="
- "Stir in poppy seeds lightly, just enough so they’re visible. Overmixing kills tender crumb, gives chewiness. Resist urge to smooth."
- "===Fill Pans ==="
- "Spoon into cups about 3/4 full, no more or they'll overflow. Level tops with spoon for uniform cooking."
- "===Bake & Watch==="
- "Place in oven. Between 14 and 18 minutes, keep eye on tops. This batter’s sugar and acid combo darkens quickly. Watch for tops that spring back under gentle press — quick finger test. If dent stays, more baking needed. Take out before edges brown hard; those get dry fast. Muffins will pop with small cracks when ready."
- "===Cool Before Diving In==="
- "Let rest 10 minutes in tins, then move to rack to cool fully or still warm, depending on craving. Sharp lemon aroma hits you first when they cool."
