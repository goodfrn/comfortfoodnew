ingredients:
- "2 cups heavy whipping cream"
- "1 cup half and half"
- "2/3 cup brown sugar"
- "1 whole vanilla bean"
- "Pinch of kosher salt"
instructions:
- "=== Mix liquids and sugar ==="
- "In a medium saucepan, stir together heavy cream, half and half, and brown sugar. No rushing. Sugar should dissolve gradually, no grit."
- "=== Split and savage vanilla bean ==="
- "Slice the vanilla bean lengthwise, scrape out sticky specks (caviar). Throw both pod and seeds into saucepan. That aroma, heady and warm, signals flavor release."
- "=== Heat gently ==="
- "Warm mixture over medium-low heat. Watch carefully — tiny steam wisps, small bubbles form on edges. No boiling! Boil spells separation and ruin."
- "=== Remove, salt, and chill ==="
- "Pull off heat at first bubble frenzy. Stir in a pinch of kosher salt; it cuts sweetness and deepens flavor layers. Let cool almost fully on counter, then seal in airtight container. Refrigerate 3 to 48 hours minimum. Patience pays."
- "=== Prep and churn ==="
- "Setup your ice cream maker per manufacturer’s manual. Pour chilled custard in. Expect a gentle hum, slushy cold thickening. Stop when texture is soft, like creamy mousse."
- "=== Freeze for finish ==="
- "Transfer gelato to sealed container. Freeze an extra few hours until scoopable but not hard stone. Check often—overfreeze dulls flavors."
- "=== Share the tale ==="
- "Had a past stumble with icy or bland? Longer chilling plus brown sugar upgrade knocked issues dead. Tell your stories in the comments."
