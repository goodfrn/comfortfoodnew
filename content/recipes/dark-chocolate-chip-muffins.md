ingredients:
- "1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour"
- "1/3 cup unsweetened dark cocoa powder"
- "3/4 cup granulated sugar"
- "1/4 tsp fine sea salt"
- "1 tsp baking soda"
- "2 large eggs"
- "1 cup sour cream"
- "1/2 cup whole milk"
- "1/3 cup vegetable oil"
- "1 tsp pure vanilla extract"
- "1 cup semi-sweet dark chocolate chips"
instructions:
- "Preheat oven to 380 degrees Fahrenheit. Line muffin tin with paper liners or grease well for easier release."
- "In a big bowl, sift together flour, dark cocoa, sugar, salt, and baking soda. Breaking lumps of cocoa powder with fingertips helps avoid streaks in batter."
- "In a separate bowl, whisk eggs vigorously until yolks and whites just blend. Add sour cream, milk, oil, and vanilla. Whisk gently to combine but keep air in mix."
- "Add chocolate chips directly to dry flour mixture and toss lightly to coat. Pour wet mixture over dry. Fold carefully with spatula just until barely incorporated. Overmix and your crumb will toughen."
- "Fill liners about 3/4 full. Spoon batter evenly. Look for surface with faint peaks from lift of batter. Smooth tops flatten during bake."
- "Slide into oven. Bake 17 to 20 minutes. Jiggling pan helps check doneness; muffin tops should spring back lightly but still feel soft. Toothpick will come out with moist crumbs not wet batter."
- "Cool in pan 5 minutes then transfer to rack. Storing airtight keeps edges from drying but best eaten within 2 days for freshness."
