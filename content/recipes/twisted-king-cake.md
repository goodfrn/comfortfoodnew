ingredients:
- "2 cans crescent roll dough"
- "3 tablespoons cinnamon sugar mix"
- "3 ounces softened cream cheese"
- "1 packet powdered sugar"
- "1 tablespoon lemon zest"
- "Colored sugar sprinkles in purple green gold"
instructions:
- "=== Dough prep and pan setup ==="
- "Preheat oven to 355°F. Spray a Bundt pan with non-stick spray generously. Don’t skimp here or the cake sticks like glue."
- "Unroll crescent dough sheets and pinch seams together forming one big sheet. Cut into strips about 1 inch wide."
- "Brush each strip lightly with melted butter, then sprinkle cinnamon sugar evenly. Roll each strip loosely to preserve flaky layers."
- "Line the Bundt pan bottom and walls by placing rolled dough strips, squeezing gently, filling all around to build a thick ring shape. Overlaps help it hold during rising and baking."
- "=== Baking phase ==="
- "Slide pan into oven; listen for soft crackles as edges brown. Around 27-30 minutes, the dough should puff up and edges darken to golden brown—not too dark."
- "If dough looks pale but feels springy, bake 3-5 minutes more. Use a toothpick peek to check—no gooey dough should stick."
- "Remove, let cool 15 minutes in pan. If sticking, gently run a thin butter knife around edges before inverting carefully onto platter."
- "=== Icing and finishing ==="
- "While cooling, mix cream cheese with powdered sugar and lemon zest until thick but spreadable. The lemon zest cuts richness, balancing the sugary glaze."
- "Spread icing thickly over cooled cake surface, smoothing some drips down sides."
- "Sprinkle purple, green, gold sugars in neat alternating lines or blotches, depending on mood."
- "Let set 10 minutes or serve immediately—icing is creamy but firm. Store leftovers wrapped tightly; best eaten day of for crispness."
- "=== Tips and fixes ==="
- "If dough seams tear, pinch tightly or fold extra dough to patch; dough expands during baking so leaks create tough spots."
- "Replace cream cheese with mascarpone for tangier finish. Swap lemon zest for orange if preferred citrus."
- "If pan sticks, chill cake 10 minutes before turning out to firm crumb and ease release."
- "Sugars melt quickly; sprinkle last minute to avoid dull colors."
- "Don’t overbake—the cinnamon sugar should caramelize lightly but not burn."
