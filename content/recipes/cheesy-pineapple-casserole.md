ingredients:
- "1 20oz can pineapple tidbits drained, reserve 8 Tbsp juice"
- "1 cup shredded sharp cheddar cheese"
- "3 Tbsp brown sugar"
- "3 Tbsp all-purpose flour"
- "8 Tbsp reserved pineapple juice"
- "1 1/2 cups crushed wheat crackers"
- "5 Tbsp melted unsalted butter"
instructions:
- "Preheat oven to 360F, spray 9x9 glass or ceramic dish lightly with nonstick spray. Set aside."
- "Drain pineapple tidbits thoroughly—don’t skip. Add fruit to a large bowl."
- "Fold in shredded cheddar cheese. Sharpness balances sweetness, don’t dilute with mild cheese."
- "In a small bowl, whisk together brown sugar and flour evenly."
- "Slowly incorporate reserved pineapple juice, whisk until silky smooth no lumps lurking."
- "Pour liquid batter over pineapple-cheese and gently stir to combine. Aim for even distribution but don’t overmix."
- "In separate bowl, toss crushed wheat crackers with melted butter until crumbs uniformly coated and glistening."
- "Spread pineapple mixture evenly in dish bottom. Pat down gently to compact without crushing fruit."
- "Scatter buttery crumbs evenly atop fruit layer. Important: crumb layer must look thick, dense—adds crunch and contrast."
- "Bake for 25 to 30 minutes. Watch for crackers turning from pale beige to golden brown, cracking softly, bubbling edges. Insert toothpick near center; if clean or slightly sticky, done."
- "Pull from oven, let rest 5 minutes—top crisps further, filling settles."
- "Serve warm. Refrigerate leftovers tightly covered; best eaten within 2 days."
