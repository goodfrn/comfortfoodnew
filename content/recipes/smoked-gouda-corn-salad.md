ingredients:
- "4 ears fresh corn, kernels cut off"
- "3 ripe medium tomatoes, chopped"
- "4 scallions thinly sliced"
- "7 ounces smoked cheddar cheese, diced"
- "1/3 cup zesty Italian dressing"
- "1/2 teaspoon salt, adjust to taste"
- "1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper"
instructions:
- "Slice kernels from corn cobs into a large bowl; watch for stubborn pulp and scrape gently so you get maximum bite."
- "Add the tomatoes, scallions, diced smoked cheddar, and pour in Italian dressing; salt and pepper follow."
- "Toss vigorously—feel the textures blend; dressing coats kernels and cheese settles in."
- "Cover bowl tightly; chill 50-65 minutes, let flavors marry but don’t overdo or tomatoes break down."
- "Before serving, toss again to wake flavors and redistribute dressing; serve cold or just off chill."
