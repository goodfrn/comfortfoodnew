ingredients:
- "3 large eggs"
- "1 cup granulated sugar or substitute light brown sugar"
- "2 teaspoons clear vanilla extract, or try vanilla bean paste"
- "¼ teaspoon salt"
- "10 tablespoons unsalted butter"
- "4 ounces chopped white chocolate, good quality bars preferred"
- "¾ cup all-purpose flour"
- "For frosting"
- "6 tablespoons unsalted butter, softened"
- "2 cups powdered sugar, divided"
- "2 tablespoons heavy cream"
- "1 teaspoon clear vanilla extract"
- "½ teaspoon almond extract"
- "Pinch salt"
instructions:
- "Preheat oven to 355°F. Line 8x8-inch pan with parchment paper leaving plenty hanging over sides—helps with clean removal later, trust me."
- "Whisk eggs, sugar, vanilla, and salt lightly in small bowl—no need for full aeration, just combined and shiny."
- "Melt butter and chopped white chocolate in a medium saucepan over medium-low. Stir constantly, listen for gentle sizzle, shut off heat just when fully melted and thickened. No burnt spots, no lumps—if grainy, remove from heat and stir longer off flame. Fail here, use microwave in bursts (15 sec)."
- "Add flour all at once to butter-chocolate. Use spatula to fold until thick batter forms—don’t overmix; you want dense, not cake-like. Texture should pull away from pan slightly."
- "Combine egg mixture into chocolate-flour mix. Whisk until homogenous. Thick but pourable. You’ll see a pale golden hue, that’s good."
- "Pour batter into parchment-lined pan, smooth tops but don’t press. Bake 21-24 minutes. Start checking at 20 with toothpick poke—little moist crumbs cling but no raw batter. Edges will firm, center gently set but spring back to touch, not gooey like fudge."
- "Cool fully on a wire rack. Impatience here ruins frosting later—warm brownies soak it up or melt frosting."
- "Frosting: Beat softened butter with handheld mixer on med speed till fluffy, about 1-2 minutes, light color and airy texture important here."
- "Add 1 cup powdered sugar, mix 30-45 seconds. Add 1 tablespoon heavy cream; beat 1 minute till silky."
- "Add remaining powdered sugar; beat 1 minute more. Should thicken again, fluffy but spreadable."
- "Add remaining cream, vanilla, almond extract, pinch salt. Go med-high speed 2-3 minutes till light, airy, no graininess. Taste frequently, adjust cream for softness."
- "Spread frosting over cooled brownies with offset spatula—thin, even layer or generous dollops if you dare. Chill briefly if too soft."
- "Slice using warmed knife for clean edges."
