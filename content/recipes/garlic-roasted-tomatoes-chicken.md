- "1 1/2 pounds tomatoes firm but ripe"
- "6 cloves garlic peeled whole"
- "3 tablespoons olive oil"
- "1 teaspoon salt divided"
- "1/4 teaspoon white pepper divided"

- "1 cup all-purpose flour"
- "2 large eggs beaten"
- "1 1/4 cups bread crumbs plain or panko"
- "1/3 cup grated Parmesan cheese"
- "4 chicken breasts thin pounded"
- "1 inch vegetable oil for frying"

- "Balsamic glaze or reduction for drizzling"
- "Fresh basil handful leaves (optional twist, swapped for oregano)"
instructions:
- "=== Make Roast Tomatoes Garlic ==="
- "Heat oven to 290°F low and slow. Quarter tomatoes, scrub woody stems off. Rip out juicy pulp seeds stubbornly with fingers or spoon for less soggy roast. Skins optional — if bothered, blanch peel first, but I don’t bother fussing here. Toss tomatoes cloves garlic in olive oil salt pepper spread out single layer on foil-lined rimmed tray. Roast hour check every 12 mins let steam escape — lids trap moisture dull browning. Tomatoes flatten, slightly shriveled edges deepening scent, garlic softens till almost buttery. Dice tomatoes gently, scoop garlic whole, collect basil or oregano and juices into bowl. Set aside or strain skins now if needed — I leave skins for rustic texture."
- "=== Prepare Chicken Cutlets ==="
- "Set oven to 205°F or lowest warm setting. Setup breading stations — flour nutmeg salt pepper mix in plate one; beaten eggs simple bowl two; bread crumb Parmesan blend third plate. Coat chicken breasts, first flour dust for sticky base, quick egg dip for glue, heavy bread crumb pressing with fingers to really stick - key for crunch hold on frying. Rest 12 minutes lets crust settle not slide off. While resting heat one inch vegetable oil medium-high pan – test drop part of crumb should bubble aggressively but not burn fast. Fry chicken flat no crowding, 4 minutes each side rich golden brown crust forming crackling sounds. Do not flip too soon or crust tears. Drain on wire rack paper towel avoids soggy bath. Transfer finished breasts to warm oven rack to hold crisp without drying."
- "=== Assembly ==="
- "Scatter arugula or spinach sharp green bed, plate juicy cutlets on top. Spoon roasted tomato pieces garlic cloves and juices over and around. Final rain balsamic glaze for acid balancing sweet brightness. Basil or oregano sprinkle adds fresh earthiness. Serve hot offsetting crunch tender chicken with silky garlic and tangy roasted tomatoes."
- "=== Notes & Tips ==="
- "Nutmeg subtle but elevates breading, no substitute needed though grated mace close. Cheese bring umami and melds breadcrumbs crisp better than plain. If breadcrumbs clump or fall off, skip resting or press crumbs harder. Avoid crowded frying; temperature drops greedy oil sucks heat make greasy chicken. Holding chicken in too hot oven risks drying — low and slow keeps texture crisp soft warm. Tomatoes release lot of water, pulling seeds+ pulp stops watery roast you want concentrated flavor. Garlic cloves roast soft mellow, can mash into tomatoes for spreadable addition. Oregano swap brings woodsy aroma over basil if pantry empty. Balsamic glaze store-bought or reduced from vinegar plus honey simmered thick. If you lack wire rack, drain on doubled paper towels and flip often to avoid steam sogginess. For gluten-free, swap flour bread crumbs with cornstarch and almond meal respectively; skip nutmeg if almond meal spices overlap oddly."
