ingredients:
- "1 thawed tube of cinnamon roll dough"
- "3/4 cup cherry preserves instead of strawberry pie filling"
- "1/3 cup sugar"
- "1 tablespoon ground cinnamon"
- "1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg"
- "1/3 cup heavy cream"
- "6 ounces cream cheese, softened"
- "1 cup powdered sugar"
- "1 tsp vanilla extract"
- "zest of 1 small lemon"
instructions:
- "Preheat oven to 345F. Why lower than usual? Dry edges show too fast at 350F."
- "Lightly flour board or parchment. Roll dough into rough 12 x 18 inch rectangle. No perfect edges. Aim for thickness around 1/4 inch. Too thin, filling leaks; too thick, dough chewy."
- "In a bowl mix sugar cinnamon nutmeg. More cinnamon slows sweetness balance. Nutmeg adds warmth."
- "Spread cherry preserves evenly but not too thick. Avoid watery puddles that bog dough. Use spatula but leave 1/2 inch dough margin."
- "Sprinkle sugar spice mix evenly on top of preserves. Press lightly for some adhesion but no soaking dough."
- "Roll up dough tightly from long edge. Tight roll means better spiral definition; loose means collapse in oven."
- "Cut roll into 12 pieces, about 1 3/4 inch thick—not thinner. Thinner burns and dries quickly."
- "Place rolls in greased 9x13 dish with equal spaces. One inch apart to allow rise expansion."
- "Cover with thin towel or plastic wrap sprayed with oil. Warm spot with no breeze or fridge noise. I use top of fridge."
- "Let rise 1 to 1 1/2 hours until puffy but not doubled. Dough should spring back slowly when poked gently."
- "Pour heavy cream over rolls before baking. Cream keeps tops moist, adds richness, and prevents crust cracking."
- "Bake 20-25 minutes at 345F. Watch color—edges golden, fruit bubbly, dough firm but springs to touch."
- "Remove when top jiggles slightly but doesn't sink when nudged. Overbaking dries rolls."
- "Beat cream cheese frosting: cream cheese, powdered sugar, vanilla, lemon zest. Lemon zest cuts rich tang. Skip if too tart."
- "Spread frosting on warm rolls for best melt-in effect."
- "Serve warm. Rolls should pull apart sticky strings of fruit and sugar, soft dough inside with slight chew of crust."
- "Leftovers reheat with damp towel in microwave 20 seconds. Avoids drying crust."
