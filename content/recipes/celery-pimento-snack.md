instructions:
- "Trim celery rinsed under cold water. Dry completely with towel or salad spinner. Don’t skip drying or cheese won’t stick."
- "Cut celery into 3-4 inch lengths. Chunky pieces better hold cheese without wobble."
- "Spoon softened pimento cheese over celery grooves. Not too much or it slides off. Use back of spoon to compact gently."
- "Top with paprika or chives if feeling fancy. Adds color and mild kick."
- "Chill in fridge 10 minutes minimum so cheese firms up. Vital step—keeps snack neat at first bite."
- "If celery loses crunch, douse pieces in ice water 10 minutes before starting. Revives them instantly."
- "Dip ready celery into extra cheese spread for double flavor. Bold move, but rewarding."
- "If celery is bitter or stringy, peel outer layers away before slicing. Texture upgrade guaranteed."
- "Serve cold but not freezer chilled. Cheese firms; celery snaps. Contrast evident visually and texturally."
