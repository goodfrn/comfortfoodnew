ingredients:
- "1 pre-made graham cracker crust (metal tray with removable plastic lid)"
- "3/4 cup sweetened shredded coconut (can swap for unsweetened + sprinkle sugar if preferred)"
- "1 packet coconut cream instant pudding mix (3.4-ounce size)"
- "1 packet vanilla instant pudding mix (3.4-ounce size)"
- "2 cups whole milk (can use 2% or coconut milk for twist)"
- "1 teaspoon coconut extract (vanilla extract for subtler flavor)"
- "8 ounces extra creamy whipped topping (like Cool Whip, thawed)"
instructions:
- "Lift off the plastic lid, keep the crust inside that metal tray. Put that tray onto a big plate. Supports it while chilling and moving it around later."
- "Toast the shredded coconut in a dry skillet on medium-high. Stir, stir, watch it change from white to a light tan, smell that toasty aroma and crunchiness start. About 3-5 minutes, don’t wander or risk burnt bitter flakes."
- "Dump toasted coconut onto a plate to cool. Don’t skip this or your topping will wilt from residual heat."
- "Slap both pudding packets into a large bowl. Pour in 2 cups whole milk and the teaspoon of coconut extract. Whisk vigorously for roughly 2-3 minutes. Look for that soft-set stage, thicker, no lumps lingering. If it feels runny still, keep going."
- "Fold in half the whipped topping (4 ounces, half tub). Use a rubber spatula. Gentle folding to avoid deflating. Keeps the pie filling light but creamy."
- "Spread this luscious coconut pudding mixture into your crust, smooth the surface with an offset spatula or the back of a spoon; makes for cleaner slices later."
- "Now take the remaining whipped topping, spread it evenly over the whole top, a solid whipped layer that’ll hold the garnish and keep the pie moist."
- "Sprinkle the cooled toasted coconut over the whipped cream top. Don’t pile too thick; you want to see layers and hear a crunch."
- "Cover loosely with plastic wrap and chill for at least 6 hours. Overnight is best. The crust will set, filling firm up just right."
- "Slice cold. Use a warm knife to get cleaner cuts. Otherwise, filling may smear or crack."
- "If lacking a premade crust, crush graham crackers with melted butter, pack firmly in pan, chill until firm. Could substitute digestive biscuits or vanilla wafers."
- "Extra twist? Stir in finely chopped pineapple or lemon zest into pudding for tropical zing. Another idea, sprinkle toasted almonds along with coconut for texture pop."
