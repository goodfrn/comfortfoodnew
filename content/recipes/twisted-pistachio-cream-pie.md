ingredients:
- "1 ready-made graham cracker crust with plastic insert"
- "1 box instant pistachio pudding mix (about 3.4 oz)"
- "1½ cups whole milk or almond milk for twist"
- "8 ounces softened cream cheese"
- "1 cup marshmallow fluff"
- "8 ounces extra creamy whipped topping, thawed and divided"
- "½ cup finely chopped pistachio nuts, divided"
- "Optional twist: 2 tablespoons coconut yogurt to fold in"
- "Optional garnish: extra chopped pistachios or toasted slices"
instructions:
- "Pull out plastic insert from graham crust gently; set aside to use as a lid later — key for keeping pie fresh."
- "In a medium bowl, whisk instant pistachio pudding mix with 1½ cups milk vigorously for 1 ½ to 2 ½ minutes — watch it thicken and bulge slightly, like slow waves in pudding texture."
- "In a large bowl, beat softened cream cheese with marshmallow fluff using handheld mixer on medium. Aim for fluffiness without lumps — takes 2 to 3 minutes; scrape sides to avoid clumps."
- "Add thickened pistachio pudding, half the whipped topping (about 4 oz plus a smidge), and ¼ cup of chopped pistachios. Fold gently but thoroughly. Incorporate optional coconut yogurt now if using."
- "Spread filling evenly into crust. Tap edges of crust on counter to settle filling flat — look for no peaks or valleys on surface."
- "Chill pie at least 3 ½ hours but ideally 4 to 6; wrinkles appear slightly on filling's surface, signaling setting. Can refrigerate overnight—easier slice, firmer bites."
- "Pipe remaining whipped topping around pie’s edge using piping bag or sturdy zip-top with tip cut. Don’t skip this step—the contrast in texture and appearance is worth it."
- "Sprinkle remaining ¼ cup chopped pistachios on top. Toasted nuts add nuttier aroma and crunch if preferred."
- "Keep plastic insert ready for cover when refrigerating to avoid pie drying or picking up fridge odors."
