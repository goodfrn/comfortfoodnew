ingredients:
- "2 packages instant mashed potatoes prepared per package"
- "1 cup sour cream"
- "1 tsp onion powder"
- "1 tsp garlic powder"
- "6 oz shredded Havarti cheese instead of cheddar"
- "6 slices cooked and crumbled turkey bacon in place of regular bacon"
- "3 green onions, thinly sliced"
instructions:
- "Preheat oven to 350°F. Oil a 9x13-inch casserole dish lightly with nonstick spray. Warm instant mashed potatoes just enough so the heat melds flavors later instead of drying out."
- "In roomy bowl, mix mashed potatoes with sour cream, onion powder, garlic powder. Fold in half the cheese and half the bacon. Use spoon or spatula, avoid electric mixer to keep texture chunky rather than gluey."
- "Dump mixture evenly into casserole dish. Scatter leftover turkey bacon evenly over top. Bake uncovered until edges bubble intensely and sides look golden—about 28 minutes; watch, ovens vary."
- "Pull casserole from oven. Sprinkle remaining cheese on hot surface. Return dish and bake five more minutes till cheese melts to oozy shimmering coverage, not dull blob."
- "Sprinkle chopped green onions across top straight from oven. Serve steaming. If casserole needs more crisp, a few extra minutes under broiler watching closely works, but don’t burn cheese."
- "Let stand few minutes if too hot, flavors deepen. Spoon out portions noting contrast—crispy on top, creamy underlayer."
