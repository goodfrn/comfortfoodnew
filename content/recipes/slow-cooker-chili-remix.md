instructions:
- "Beef in the skillet. Medium high. Break it up. No pink. Drain the fat."

- "Both go in the slow cooker."
- "Black beans, tomatoes, tomato paste, broth, all the spices. In. Stir it properly. No dry pockets."
- "High heat. Three hours forty five minutes. Don't stare at it. Check edges around three hours — bubbling slow, smells smoky. That's it working."
- "Too watery near the end — lid off. Fifteen minutes uncovered fixes it."
- "Taste before serving. Salt if it needs it."
- "Bowl. Cheddar. Sour cream. Scallions. Done."
