ingredients:
- "2 cups water"
- "2 tablespoons avocado oil"
- "1 ½ cups long grain white rice"
- "1 cup canned diced tomatoes"
- "1 teaspoon ground cumin"
- "½ teaspoon garlic powder"
- "½ teaspoon onion powder"
- "½ teaspoon salt"
- "½ teaspoon chili powder"
instructions:
- "===Preparation==="
- "Pour 2 cups water into wide saucepan. Bring to rolling boil—listen for that aggressive bubble dance, no faint simmer here."
- "Add 2 tablespoons avocado oil. Swirl immediately; this coats grains, prevents sticking. No oil, rice clumps detectably."
- "Dump 1 ½ cups rice. Stir briskly—ensures grains separate, toast slightly in hot liquid before it re-boils. The sizzle should sharpen."
- "Toss 1 cup canned diced tomatoes plus cumin, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, chili powder. Stir once carefully but quickly. Tomatoes add moisture, mild tang over salsa’s punch."
- "===Cooking==="
- "Lower heat to bare simmer—a gentle roar, not silence. Cover with tight-fitting lid, trapping steam. Resist urge to flick lid up—kills puffing rice."
- "After around 12 minutes, open lid briefly. Peek for liquid level; grains should be swelling, with some water still clinging. Stir gently once. Helps even cooking but rough agitation bruises grains."
- "Reseal lid, simmer another 10 minutes. Between 22-25 minutes, test rice by mashing a grain with spoon tip—should be tender but not falling apart."
- "If still chewy, add splash water, cover again briefly. Remember, moist snug grain, but no swimming pool."
- "===Finishing==="
- "Remove from heat. Let rest covered 5 minutes for steam to redistribute evenly. Fluff with fork—dry rice fluffs easily, sticky rice fights back."
- "Smell should be earthy with toasted aroma, faint tomato sweetness, mild cumin heat."
- "Serve immediately or cool for reheating. Reheat sprinkled with water to restore moisture; microwave with damp paper towel sealed keeps it fluffy."
- "===Troubleshooting==="
- "Burnt bottom? Next batch use medium-low heat after boiling. Use heavier pot if possible, rotates heat better."
- "Too dry? Less simmer time or more liquid next attempt."
- "Too mushy? Less liquid or shorter resting time."
- "Tomatoes too watery? Drain some juice or use thicker paste instead."
- "Avocado oil stands up better under heat than olive oil without overpowering flavor."
