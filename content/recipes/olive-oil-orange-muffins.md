ingredients:
- "1 cup all-purpose flour"
- "1 cup sprouted wheat flour"
- "1 teaspoon dried rosemary"
- "1/2 teaspoon salt"
- "1 teaspoon baking powder"
- "1/2 teaspoon baking soda"
- "2 large eggs"
- "1/3 cup extra-virgin olive oil"
- "1/2 cup whole milk"
- "1/3 cup fresh orange juice"
- "1 tablespoon orange zest"
- "1/4 teaspoon ground cardamom"
- "Orange slices for topping"
instructions:
- "=== Preparation ==="
- "Heat the oven to 345 degrees Fahrenheit. Lip of muffin tin sprayed with nonstick spray; you can also butter and flour but spray speeds cleanup."
- "=== Dry Ingredients ==="
- "Mix the flours, rosemary, salt, baking powder, baking soda, and cardamom thoroughly. Break any clumps, sift if needed for even rise."
- "=== Wet Ingredients ==="
- "Beat eggs lightly. Stir in olive oil, milk, orange juice, and zest. Whisk until looks uniform but don’t aerate too much."
- "=== Combining ==="
- "Dump wet right into dry. Fold gently, only until no dry patches. Overmix — dense, rubbery muffins. Resist the urge to beat that last speck of flour."
- "=== Portioning and Baking ==="
- "Use a 3/4 cup measure to fill each cup fairly full. Place 3-4 quartered fresh orange slices atop each muffin; they add burst and caramelize slightly."
- "Bake about 23 minutes. Look for golden edges and just firm tops. Toothpick test: a few moist crumbs cling but no wet batter. Oven temps vary, so watch the color and springiness. Cool 10 minutes. Muffins should yield slightly under fingertip pressure but not collapse."
- "=== Serving and Storage ==="
- "Enjoy warm or cooled. Store airtight, last 2 days best. Refresh 5 minutes in 350 oven to perk the crust."
- "=== Troubleshooting ==="
- "If muffins slump, probably overmixed or too much juice. Add an extra tablespoon flour next round. Substitute sprouted flour for oat or whole wheat but lower quantity to avoid heaviness."
- "No fresh oranges? Use bottled juice zest the peel of a lemon for similar zing. Don’t skip olive oil; it keeps crumb tender unlike butter here."
- "Want sweeter? A splash honey in wet. Craving crunch? Toss pepitas or chopped nuts in dry half cup max."
