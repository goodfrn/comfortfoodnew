ingredients:
- "4 large egg whites"
- "1/4 teaspoon cream of tartar"
- "1 cup granulated sugar"
- "1 teaspoon vanilla extract"
instructions:
- "Preheat oven to 210 degrees F. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper, wrinkles okay but flat enough to prevent pool of egg whites"
- "In a clean, dry large bowl, beat egg whites and cream of tartar on medium speed until foamy bubbles appear"
- "Increase speed, slowly sprinkle in sugar in three additions, whipping until mixture holds stiff peaks and sugar feels dissolved when rubbed between fingers"
- "Fold in vanilla extract gently but thoroughly"
- "Scoop six large mounds of meringue onto parchment; shape each mound into a thick disc about 3 inches wide"
- "Using the back of a spoon, carve a bowl shape in the center of each mound, but don’t pierce to baking sheet or thin base will burn"
- "Place trays in oven, bake for 65 minutes; meringues should sound hollow when tapped and show slight ivory hue rather than bright white"
- "Turn off oven, leave meringues inside for 1 hour or more to cool slowly, avoiding cracks caused by abrupt temperature changes"
- "Remove and store airtight until ready to fill. Fill bowls with ice cream and drizzle sauce just before serving"
