ingredients:
- "11/8 cups all-purpose flour"
- "3/4 cup sugar"
- "1/3 cup unsweetened cocoa powder"
- "3/4 teaspoon baking soda"
- "1/2 teaspoon salt"
- "1 cup water"
- "1/3 cup vegetable oil (canola or light olive oil works)"
- "1 tablespoon white or apple cider vinegar"
- "1 teaspoon vanilla extract"
- "8 ounces cream cheese, softened"
- "10 tablespoons granulated sugar (reduce from 12 tablespoons)"
- "1 large egg"
- "3/4 teaspoon vanilla extract"
- "1/3 cup mini semisweet chocolate chips (sub dark for twist)"
instructions:
- "Heat oven to 345°F. Line two muffin tins, about 22 liners total. Paper liners only; foil causes uneven baking."
- "Whip cream cheese and sugar together in a medium bowl with handheld mixer on med-high for 40 seconds, no lumps remaining. Add egg and 3/4 tsp vanilla, blend just until no streaks, avoid over mixing or it’ll crack while baking. Fold in chocolate chips gently but fully. Set aside."
- "In large bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, cocoa, baking soda, and salt. Make sure to sift cocoa powder for no clumps."
- "Add water, oil, vinegar, and 1 tsp vanilla to dry mix. Whisk briskly until batter is uniform and slightly thick but pourable. Over mixing can cause dense cupcakes. Watch for batter sheen and swirl."
- "Portion batter into liners filling roughly one third full—about 1 1/2 tablespoons each. Dollop 2 teaspoons cream cheese mix in center of each. Don’t overfill or cream cheese sinks."
- "Bake 18-21 minutes. Start checking at 18. Use toothpick; if inserted into cake portion it comes out clean, done. Ignore toothpick if it has cream cheese on it. Cupcake tops turn shiny, feel just set to touch but spring back slightly."
- "Cool in pans 8-10 minutes; cups will firm up as steam escapes. Transfer to wire rack for full cool. Room temp ensures cream cheese sets without sweating."
- "Store chilled up to 3 days. Freeze airtight up to 2 months. Defrost in fridge overnight; warm to room temp before serving for best texture."
