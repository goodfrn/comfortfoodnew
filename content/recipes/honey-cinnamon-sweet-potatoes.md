ingredients:
- "4 cups sweet potatoes diced approx 1 inch"
- "3 tablespoons unsalted butter or coconut oil as alternative"
- "2 tablespoons honey or maple syrup for twist"
- "1 teaspoon ground cinnamon"
- "½ teaspoon salt"
- "¼ teaspoon freshly cracked black pepper"
instructions:
- "1 Heat oven to 355°F, toss sweet potato cubes with no oil yet."
- "2 Warm a heavy skillet, preferably cast iron, on medium-high heat until shimmering but not smoking."
- "3 Drop butter in, let it melt fully till foamy and smelling nutty but not burnt."
- "4 Add potatoes in a single layer, resist crowding the pan. Sear until a golden brown crust forms on at least 2 sides, maybe 5-7 minutes. You want browning, not softness yet."
- "5 Sprinkle cinnamon, salt and pepper right over potatoes, swirl gently to disperse seasoning. Next, drizzle honey evenly to coat. Listen for soft sizzle."
- "6 Transfer skillet straight to the oven. Bake uncovered about 15-20 minutes. Doneness is when you pierce a cube easily with a fork and it yields but still holds shape. Look for slight caramelization on edges."
- "7 Remove from oven, smell deep buttery sweetness melded with warm spices. Serve immediately for best texture and aroma."
