ingredients:
- "2 teaspoons ground cumin"
- "2 teaspoons ground coriander"
- "1 teaspoon white pepper"
- "1 teaspoon ground cinnamon"
- "3/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg"
- "1 1/4 teaspoons sea salt"
- "6 lamb loin chops"
- "2 tablespoons olive oil"
- "3/4 cup pomegranate juice"
- "1 tablespoon cornstarch"
- "2 tablespoons water"
- "1 1/2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar"
- "1 1/2 teaspoons granulated sugar"
- "2 tablespoons unsalted butter"
- "3 tablespoons pomegranate arils"
instructions:
- "Mix cumin, coriander, white pepper, cinnamon, nutmeg, and sea salt in a small bowl until evenly combined. Avoid clumps; dry spices blend better when whisked."
- "Pat lamb chops completely dry with paper towels. Any moisture means no crust, just steam."
- "Stamp spice mix onto both sides of each chop. More pressure, deeper flavor penetration. Let them rest 10 minutes at room temperature if time allows."
- "Heat a heavy skillet (cast iron preferred) over medium heat until shimmering but not smoking. Add olive oil, swirl to coat."
- "Lay chops in the pan without moving. No flipping too soon. Wait for a deep, dark crust to form—about 4-5 minutes. Look for rich brown color and occasional ‘pop’ sound."
- "Flip and repeat on the other side, 4 minutes again. Use tongs, gentle press with fingertips; should feel springy but not squishy. Target an internal temp just below 145°F—carryover cooks the rest."
- "Lift chops out, tent with foil, rest 7-9 minutes on warmed plate or rack. Resting seals juices."
- "Discard excess fat but keep cooked bits stuck in pan for flavor. Pour off surplus oil."
- "Pour pomegranate juice into pan, simmer gently over low heat. Scrape browned bits with wooden spoon—they infuse sauce."
- "Whisk cornstarch with cold water to make slurry. Slowly stir it into juice along with apple cider vinegar and sugar. Sauce will thicken in 2-3 minutes, watch closely to avoid lumps."
- "Off heat, stir in butter cubes piece by piece, swirl pan until glossy smooth. Adds silk and sheen."
- "Plate lamb chops, spoon about 2 tablespoons of pomegranate sauce over each. Scatter arils on top—burst of tart crunch."
- "Serve immediately. If lamb unavailable, thick-cut pork loin chops cooked same way work well. For thicker sauce, add more slurry; for sweeter, reduce vinegar slightly."
