ingredients:
- "1 cup honey"
- "1 tablespoon crushed red pepper flakes"
- "2 teaspoons apple cider vinegar"
- "1/2 teaspoon kosher salt"
instructions:
- "Pour honey, crushed red pepper flakes, apple cider vinegar, and kosher salt into a small saucepan."
- "Set stove to low heat. Stir the mix occasionally; watch as the honey loosens but don’t let it bubble or simmer - bubbles mean too hot."
- "Listen for soft bubbling sounds to pause; if it crackles, pull back heat. About 10-15 minutes here, but trust sensory cues not the clock."
- "Look for thick, shiny texture that flows easier than raw honey. Aroma will shift from pure honey sweetness to a spice-kissed scent."
- "Remove pan from heat. Let cool completely in the pan; it thickens as it drops in temperature."
- "Optional: Strain through fine mesh strainer to catch pepper flakes, or keep them for a rustic look and punch."
- "Transfer to a clean jar or bottle. Seal and store at room temp. Shake before every use to redistribute flavors."

- "q: Why does honey become grainy? a: Usually from rapid cooling or storing hot honey in fridge. Crystals form and thicken messes up pouring. Fix by gentle reheating and stirring slowly.
