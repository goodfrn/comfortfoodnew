- "1/3 cup almond butter"
- "1/4 cup raw honey"
- "1/4 cup vanilla whey protein"

- "1/2 teaspoon cinnamon"
- "1/4 cup mini chocolate chips"
instructions:
- "Pulse oats in food processor few times until coarsely chopped not powdery. Stops bites getting gritty but easier to mix."
- "Add almond butter, honey, protein powder, flaxseed, cinnamon. Hit high speed and watch mixture turn sticky, clumping. Stop when it just holds shape not oily or mushy."
- "If mixture looks crumbly, pinch and squeeze. Should hold under little pressure. If not add teaspoon nut butter or splash water. Pulse briefly again. Avoid over blending or it turns pasty."
- "Scoop with cookie scoop or hands. Roll firmly but don’t over-compact or texture densifies. Mini balls? More yield. Regular size, about 14."
- "Toss in chocolate chips or drizzle melted dark chocolate while still warm from microwave, swirl quickly then refrigerate. Sets fast. Keeps bites moist inside."
- "Store airtight in fridge. Will last week but honestly bite size disappears fast. Room temp for quick eat but firmer if chilled."
