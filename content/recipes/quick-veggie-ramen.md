ingredients:
- "1 cup sliced green onions"
- "2 teaspoons soy sauce"
- "1 tablespoon rice vinegar"
- "1 teaspoon toasted sesame oil"
- "1 tablespoon sesame seeds"
- "2 tablespoons sesame oil"
- "4 cloves garlic minced"
- "1 inch fresh ginger minced"
- "6 cups vegetable broth"
- "3 tablespoons low sodium soy sauce"
- "1 cup sliced shiitake mushrooms"
- "1 package ramen noodles or 2 cups spiralized zucchini"
- "2 medium carrots shredded"
- "4 soft boiled eggs"
- "1 tablespoon dried seaweed flakes"
instructions:
- "Start with soaking scallions: toss green onions, soy sauce, rice vinegar, toasted sesame oil in a small bowl. Let sit while broth simmers. Marinating softens sharp bite, layers flavor. "
- "Heat 2 tbsp sesame oil in a large pot over medium heat. Toss in minced garlic and ginger, stirring constantly. Watch carefully, 25-35 seconds until aroma hits, edges just golden. Burnt garlic smacks bitter, don’t let it. "
- "Pour in vegetable broth; listen to subtle bubble forming. Add in soy sauce, simmer gently. Toss sliced shiitake mushrooms in now. These add umami punch—swap easier if needed for white buttons but less deep flavor."
- "Once broth simmers nicely, drop ramen noodles (or spiralized zucchini if you crave something lighter). Noodles soften fast; test softness—should yield under slight bite but no mush. Usually 2-3 minutes, maybe 1 minute for zucchini. "
- "Ladle soup into bowls. Top with soaked marinated green onions, sprinkle sesame seeds, add shredded carrots for crunch. Slip in soft-boiled eggs (runny yolks give creamy contrast). Sprinkle dried seaweed flakes or toast nori bits if you prefer. "
- "Taste broth before serving; adjust soy or vinegar to sharpen or mellow. Quick fix if too salty: add splash water or a pinch sugar to balance."
- "Cleanup tip: soak ramen pot immediately, garlic oils cling stubbornly otherwise."
- "Egg shortcut: boil then plunge immediately into ice bath for perfect timing; peel gently for smooth surface."
