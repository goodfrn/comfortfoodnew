ingredients:
- "6 cups rice krispie cereal"
- "4 cups mini marshmallows"
- "1/3 cup unsalted butter"
- "1 tablespoon coconut oil"
- "1 tablespoon fine sea salt"
- "8 marshmallow Peeps (bunny shapes recommended)"
- "1/2 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips"
- "Colored sprinkles"
instructions:
- "Line a 9x13 pan tightly with parchment; set aside. Proper lining saves a scraping nightmare later."
- "Grease a large bowl and spatula with butter; this stops cereal from sticking when folding. Coat at least 3 inches of the spatula handle. Toss in cereal, set bowl nearby."
- "Melt butter and coconut oil together over LOW heat in big nonstick pot. Too hot will ruin texture and taste. Watch closely."
- "Dump half marshmallows in, stirring gently to coat in butter-oil mix. Once marshmallows puff and soften like clouds melting, add remaining marshmallows to batch. No rush here but steady heat is crucial."
- "Keep stirring softly, rhythm is key. Marshmallow mass should turn glossy and flowy but still hold subtle chunkiness. Total melt about 5–6 minutes on low is typical. Avoid white clumps."
- "Off-heat, pour marshmallow syrup over cereal. Immediately fold with buttered spatula, using gentle sweeping motions. Overmix and bites go gummy, no crunch. Aim just for coverage."
- "Transfer mix into prepared pan, using buttered fingertips or parchment to press firmly all the way to corners. Don’t mash flat. Pressing distributes marshmallow without crushing cereal structure."
- "Leave bars untouched at room temp for minimum 30 minutes until set but still pliable. This phase crucial for clean cuts later without crumble."
- "Use parchment sling to lift set bars from pan. Place on cutting surface. Use marshmallow Peep as rough measure for squares. Remove Peeps before chopping, they’ll smoosh."
- "Line pieces spaced on parchment or rack. Melt chocolate chips slowly in microwave in 20-second bursts, stirring between, to avoid seizing. Drizzle chocolate just as bars turn tacky. Add sprinkles right away."
- "Top each bar with a Peep, gently pressing to anchor but not squish. Let sit until chocolate firms, 15–20 minutes should do. Store airtight once set to keep Peeps from drying."
- "If mixing candy alternatives, swap marshmallows for 1 cup white chocolate chips plus 3 cups mini marshmallows. Coconut oil key for fluidity here."
- "Got sticky spatulas? Refrigerate for 5 minutes then scrape again; butter sets fast and reduces mess. Warmer bowls break cereal clumps better but chill slightly before pressing to pan."
- "Salty hit balances out sugary marshmallow overload. Feel free to add toasted coconut flakes or chopped nuts folded in before pressing for crunch surprise."
