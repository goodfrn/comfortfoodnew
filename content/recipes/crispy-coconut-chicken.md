ingredients:
- "1 cup toasted shredded coconut"
- "1 cup panko breadcrumbs"
- "1/4 cup all-purpose flour"
- "1/2 teaspoon garlic powder"
- "1/2 teaspoon ground ginger"
- "3/4 teaspoon salt"
- "1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper"
- "2 large eggs"
- "3 tablespoons water"
- "1 tablespoon whole grain mustard"
- "1 1/2 lbs chicken tenders"
- "Non-stick cooking spray"
- "Sauce:"
- "1/4 cup pineapple preserves"
- "2 tablespoons white vinegar"
- "2 teaspoons Dijon mustard"
instructions:
- "=== For the chicken ==="
- "Oven 425 F. Spray pan lightly. Mix coconut, panko, flour, garlic powder, ginger, salt, pepper. Crunch, spice, salt balance. Beat eggs, water, whole grain mustard for moisture and a bit of tang. Dip chicken in egg, then press into coconut mix firmly. Coating must stick. Place on pan. Optional: quick spray top for crispier look and bite."
- "Bake 17 to 19 minutes. Watch for golden edges, juices run clear. Smell nutty coconut, hear crisp crackle when done. Avoid dryness; test tightness not just time."
- "=== Sauce prep ==="
- "Whisk pineapple preserves, vinegar, Dijon mustard. Sharp-sweet balance; taste for punch. Adjust vinegar or preserves to tweak zing or sweetness."
