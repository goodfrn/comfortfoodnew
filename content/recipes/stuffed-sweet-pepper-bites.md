instructions:
- "Heat oven to 430 degrees Fahrenheit; line a baking sheet with parchment paper — prevents sticking, easy cleanup."
- "Slice each mini pepper in half lengthwise, carefully remove seeds and ribs but keep stems attached; they act as handles here."
- "Spoon about 1 to 1½ teaspoons of tomato paste in each pepper half, thicker than usual sauce, holds better during bake. No watery mess."
- "Sprinkle mozzarella cheese generously, around 1 to 1½ tablespoons per half. Too little cheese, dry; too much, spills out and burns."
- "Place 3 to 5 slices of turkey pepperoni on top. Leaner option limits grease, avoids soggy texture, more control over final crunch."
- "Bake 7 to 12 minutes. Watch cheese: bubbling, edges turning golden with tiny blister spots, signals perfect melt."
- "Remove when cheese wrinkles and makes those tiny crispy bits. Peppers soften but shouldn’t collapse; they keep bite."
- "Serve hot. If prepping ahead, can refrigerate then reheat quickly under broiler 1-2 minutes to revive crisp edges."
