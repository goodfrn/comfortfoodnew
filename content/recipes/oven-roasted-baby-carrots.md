ingredients:
- "1 lb small baby carrots, tops trimmed to 1 inch"
- "2 tbsp olive oil"
- "1 tsp coarse salt"
- "1/2 tsp freshly ground black pepper"
- "1 cup ricotta cheese"
- "1 1/2 tbsp fresh lemon juice"
- "1 tsp finely chopped fresh thyme leaves"
- "zest of 1 lemon"
- "flaky sea salt, to finish (optional)"
- "extra olive oil for drizzling"
instructions:
- "Heat oven to 360F for slightly faster and deeper roast. Toss carrots with olive oil, salt, and pepper. Arrange single layer on rimmed sheet, no crowding to avoid steaming."
- "Roast 27-30 minutes, shaking pan or flipping halfway. Look for caramelized edges and softness when pierced with fork – should slide in without resistance but not mushy."
- "While carrots roast, whisk ricotta with lemon juice and thyme until creamy and bright. Chill to let flavors marry, keeps cool against warm veggies."
- "Pile warm carrots on serving dish. Spoon dollops of ricotta mixture unevenly, rustic style. Zest lemon over all for fragrant citrus pops."
- "Finish with drizzle of olive oil, fresh cracked pepper, and flaky salt if desired to add crunch and snap to earthiness."
- "Note carrot size variance – thicker roots need more time, thinner less. Fork test trumps timer. Keep some reserved oil if carrots dry out."
- "If ricotta unavailable, swap with Greek yogurt or labneh, same tangy creaminess. Thyme can be subbed with oregano or rosemary chopped fine for herbal twist."
