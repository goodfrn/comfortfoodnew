ingredients:
- "1 cup (227 grams) unsalted butter, cut into cubes, salted butter works"
- "6 ounces (170 grams) bittersweet chocolate bar, chopped"
- "1 ⅓ cups (270 grams) light brown sugar, packed"
- "3 large eggs, room temperature"
- "1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract"
- "½ teaspoon fine sea salt"
- "1 cup (130 grams) all-purpose flour"
- "FOR FROSTING:"
- "1 cup (200 grams) granulated sugar"
- "⅓ cup (80 ml) evaporated milk"
- "4 tablespoons (57 grams) butter unsalted or salted"
- "3 large egg yolks, room temperature"
- "1 ½ cups (135 grams) sweetened shredded coconut"
- "1 cup (120 grams) toasted pecans, chopped"
- "1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract"
- "OPTIONAL:"
- "⅓ cup semi-sweet chocolate chips or chopped chocolate for drizzle"
instructions:
- "HEAT oven 350°F; line 9x13 pan with parchment, spray lightly. Butter paper corners solid prevents stick."
- "MELT butter in heavy pot, medium-low heat, slow wins. Toss chopped chocolate in once butter swells, stir gently, no scorching. Watch; silky gloss forms—don’t rush or seize."
- "OFF heat, whisk brown sugar fully in. Dissolves rise sticky sweet, grainy bites gone."
- "DROP eggs one at a time, whisk fully each before next. Don’t speed or they clump."
- "ADD vanilla and salt. Salt cuts sweetness, draws depth. Shake in flour, stir gently till just joined. Avoid overmixing; batter looks lumpy is fine."
- "POUR batter into pan, scrape every glossed last bit. Smooth top or leave rustic."
- "BAKE 33-40 minutes—edges firm, center jiggles slightly, toothpick with moist crumbs is key. Underbake means fudgy, overbake dry. Look for crackly edges pulling from paper."
- "COOL completely on wire rack, patience now seals texture."
- "FROSTING: combine sugar, evaporated milk, butter, egg yolks in medium saucepan over medium-low."
- "STIR slowly until butter melts, sugar dissolves—start gentle."
- "BRING to low simmer, stir often, rare spots stick. After simmer hit, cook about 10 minutes until thickened shiny sheen forms. Thickness shows when coating spoon neutrally, runs thick but not stiff."
- "OFF heat, stir in coconut, toasted pecans, vanilla. Mix well. Continue stirring off heat about 3 minutes—cools, thickens to spreadable fudge."
- "SPREAD topping over cooled brownies. Use offset spatula for even layer or rustic dollops."
- "OPTIONALLY drizzle melted chocolate over frosting for crunch and contrast. Let it cool until set."
- "CUT bars with sharp serrated knife—warm knife if sticking or cracking edges."
- "STORE airtight at room temp a couple days; longer refrigeration toughens texture, but topping holds well."
- "SUBSTITUTIONS: butter can be replaced with coconut oil same measure. Evaporated milk can be canned regular milk simmered down by half or use coconut milk for tropical twist. Pecans swap for walnuts or almonds. Brown sugar swap to white sugar plus 1 tsp molasses for darker tone."
