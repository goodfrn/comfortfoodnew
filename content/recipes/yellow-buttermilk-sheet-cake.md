ingredients:
- "2 large eggs"
- "2 large egg yolks"
- "1 2/3 cups buttermilk"
- "3 teaspoons vanilla extract"
- "3 1/4 cups all-purpose flour"
- "1 3/4 cups granulated sugar"
- "1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder"
- "1 1/4 teaspoons baking soda"
- "1/4 teaspoon fine salt"
- "1/2 cup unsalted butter softened"
- "1/2 cup vegetable oil"
instructions:
- "Heat oven to 345 F instead of 350 to avoid crust cracking. Rack center. Grease 9x13 pan with baking spray then dust lightly with flour. Pat edges dry if extra flour on sides. Set aside."
- "Crack eggs plus extra yolks in med bowl. Whisk fast with buttermilk and vanilla until smooth, about 1 minute. No lumps or separation. Rest."
- "Combine dry: sift flour with baking powder, baking soda, salt for uniform lift. Add sugar and mix with paddle on low speed for 30 seconds. This helps prevent sugar pockets."
- "Add softened butter chunk by chunk and oil in steady stream. Increase speed slowly so mixture creams evenly into dry. After 1 minute batter looks sandy then creamy. Switch mixer to slow, add liquid mix gradually in three additions scrapping sides often."
- "Once mixed, stop, scrape, continue beating medium speed 2 1/2 minutes until batter light, voluminous, no visible flour streaks. Avoid overmix to keep crumb tender."
- "Pour batter in prepared pan. Drop pan on counter 3-4 times to release trapped air. Don’t shake pan or batter will get dense spots."
- "Bake 30-35 minutes. Jiggle pan gently at 25 min mark to sense center wobble—should feel slightly springy but set. Insert toothpick near center after 30 min; moist crumbs, never wet batter. If too wet, add 3-5 min and check again."
- "Once done, remove from oven and cool on wire rack completely to prevent soggy bottom. Use thin offset spatula to loosen sides if necessary after 15 min."
