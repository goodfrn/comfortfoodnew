ingredients:
- "1 cup unsalted butter softened"
- "3/4 cup vegetable shortening"
- "1/4 teaspoon salt"
- "4 cups powdered sugar sifted"
- "1 teaspoon vanilla extract or almond extract"
- "2 to 3 tablespoons whole milk or water"
- "gel food coloring optional"
instructions:
- "==="
- "Toss softened butter, shortening, and salt in mixer bowl. Whip medium-high, creamy but not too fluffy. Butter should soften further, shortening melts slightly, getting a pillowy base."
- "Slowly pour powdered sugar cup by cup, scraping sides after each. Keep speed low to avoid sugar dust clouding the kitchen. The mixture thickens, dry at first, keep mixing till it feels like soft dough."
- "Add vanilla or almond essence now, crucial for flavor boost. Mix well till fully absorbed, watch gloss return indicating full combo."
- "Here’s where color or additional flavor oils come in. Use gel, not liquid. Gel holds up, doesn’t dilute. Mix just enough to swirl colors in, don’t overdo or texture breaks down."
- "If thick like frosting bricks, drip milk or water one teaspoon at time, beat after each. Watch texture relax; should be light but hold shape. Too thin means runny disaster; too thick, crackly cake topping."
- "Test by finger or spatula: hold stiff swirls but still yielding under pressure."
- "Cover bowl with damp cloth or plastic wrap to prevent crust formation if waiting before use."
