ingredients:
- "1 2/3 cups orzo pasta"
- "1 medium English cucumber diced"
- "1 cup sun-dried tomatoes chopped, packed in oil"
- "3/4 cup crumbled goat cheese (instead of feta)"
- "1/3 cup kalamata olives pitted and sliced"
- "1/2 cup chopped red onion"
- "1/2 cup fresh mint leaves chopped (instead of basil)"
- "1/4 cup fresh parsley chopped"
- "For dressing"
- "1/3 cup extra virgin olive oil"
- "3 tablespoons fresh lemon juice"
- "1 tablespoon white wine vinegar"
- "1 medium garlic clove minced"
- "1/2 teaspoon dried oregano"
- "Salt and cracked black pepper to taste"
instructions:
- "=== Cook orzo ==="
- "Bring salted water to full boil, add orzo stirred constantly for even cook. Look for al dente—still a bite but not chalky. Usually 8-10 minutes but smell nutty aroma and feel slight resistance under teeth. Drain immediately in fine sieve."
- "Rinse under cold tap fast to halt cooking. Drain well, shake dry. Set aside in bowl to cool to room temp."
- "=== Combine salad ==="
- "Add diced cucumber, chopped sun-dried tomatoes, kalamata olives, red onion, crumbled goat cheese, chopped mint and parsley directly to cooled orzo. Toss gently but thoroughly."
- "=== Make dressing ==="
- "In small bowl, whisk olive oil, lemon juice, white wine vinegar, minced garlic, oregano, salt, and pepper. Whisk till emulsified and pale yellow sheen appears. Garlic should be evenly distributed for balanced punch. Taste adjust salt or lemon as needed."
- "=== Dress salad ==="
- "Pour dressing over orzo mixture. Toss completely so every piece coated. Don’t overdress or it gets soggy. Save some dressing on side if making ahead for freshness punch later."
- "Chill salad minimum 30 minutes for flavors to meld but it can serve room temp anytime."
- "Garnish last minute with fresh mint leaves for extra aroma and vibrant color."
- "=== Tips ==="
- "If no orzo, use small shells or small elbow pasta. Rinsing crucial to stop overcooking and clumping. Use goat cheese for tang, substitute with ricotta salata or queso fresco if unavailable. Add toasted pine nuts or slivered almonds for crunch twist."
- "If salad too dry after chilling, drizzle extra olive oil and toss lightly just before serving."
- "Adjust garlic amount based on preference. Reserve chopped herbs separately to toss just before serving if worried about wilt."
- "Sun-dried tomatoes packed in oil avoid soaking—if dry ones, rehydrate in warm water 10 minutes and drain thoroughly. Avoid excess liquid in salad."
