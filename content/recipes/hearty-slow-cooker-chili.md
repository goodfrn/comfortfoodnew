ingredients:
- "1 lb ground turkey lean"
- "1 medium onion diced"
- "3 cloves garlic minced"
- "1 can 15 oz kidney beans rinsed"
- "1 can 15 oz black beans rinsed"
- "1 can 15 oz fire-roasted diced tomatoes with juice"
- "1 cup chicken broth low sodium"
- "2 tbsp tomato paste"
- "2 tbsp chili powder"
- "1 tsp smoked paprika"
- "1 tsp ground cumin"
- "1/2 tsp cayenne pepper"
- "1/2 tsp dried oregano"
- "1/4 tsp black pepper"
- "Salt to taste"
- "Shredded cheddar sour cream green onions for topping"
instructions:
- "Brown the turkey in a skillet on medium. Break it up. No pink left. Drain the fat."
- "Same pan. Onion and garlic in. Cook till soft. Three four minutes."
- "Everything goes in the slow cooker. Turkey, onion, garlic, beans, tomatoes, broth, tomato paste, all the spices. Stir it."
- "Low heat. Seven and a half hours. Don't touch it much. Check edges around hour six — bubbling slow, smells deep. That's it working."
- "Stir once or twice. Don't go crazy. Too much stirring breaks the beans."
- "Too watery near the end — take the lid off. Last thirty minutes uncovered fixes it."
- "Taste before serving. Add salt if it needs it."
- "Bowl. Cheddar on top. Sour cream. Green onions. Done."
